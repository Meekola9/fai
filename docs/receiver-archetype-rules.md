# Receiver archetype separation

Receiver archetypes use the same verified FAI testing categories, but overlapping trait combinations receive profile-specific adjustments so a reordered list of Speed, Acceleration, and Change of Direction does not produce duplicate identities.

- **Field Stretcher:** linear speed supported by strong jump output.
- **Straight Line Blur:** speed clearly separates from change of direction and jump output.
- **Route Technician:** change of direction clearly separates from linear speed.
- **Gadget Weapon:** speed, acceleration, and change of direction are closely balanced.
- **Big Body Boundary:** tall/heavy profile led by power and strength.
- **Contested Catch Freak:** tall profile with jump output clearly separating.
- **Yards-After Menace:** acceleration, power, and change of direction rise together.
- **Chain Mover:** conditioning, pursuit, and change of direction lead the profile.

These labels remain testing-profile projections. Route skill, catch skill, release technique, football IQ, and production still require film evaluation.
